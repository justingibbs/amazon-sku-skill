## 2. Approved edits

### Edit 1 — Rewrite the title for descriptive depth and scannable spec ordering

**Before:**

> Ally Studio 50 Headphones

**After (135 chars):**

> Ally Studio 50 Wired Over-Ear Studio Monitor Headphones with 50mm Drivers and 1/4 to 3.5mm Jack for Recording, Mixing, and Home Listening

**Rationale (conversion):** The current title forces shoppers to scroll for every decision-driving attribute — wired vs. wireless, over-ear vs. in-ear, driver size, jack type, intended use. Most won't. Surfacing these in the title both improves the 2-second scan decision and broadens the keyword surface that brings shoppers in.

**Competitor reference:** Audio-Technica's 70-char title (`ATH-M20x Professional Studio Monitor Headphones, Black`) leads with brand + model + form factor and converts at 4.6 across 26.7k reviews; OneOdio's 188-char title surfaces driver size and jack type inline so the spec check happens before the bullets do.

**Amazon rule citations:**
- Title may not exceed 200 chars including spaces ([Seller Forums announcement](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/seller-forums/discussions/t/b2b15728-0d43-453e-974f-59eb63f73059))
- Recommended Consumer Electronics title format — Brand + Series + Model + Form Factor + "with" Unique Thing ([CE Style Guide PDF](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf))
- Capitalize the first letter of each word except prepositions, conjunctions, and articles ([Listings PDF](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf))
- Use numerals such as "50mm" instead of spelled-out numbers ([Listings PDF](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf))

### Edit 2 — Replace 3 vague bullets with 5 specific feature-with-benefit bullets

**Before:**

- Studio sound.
- Wired connection.
- Comfortable for long sessions.

**After:**

- Studio-grade monitoring sound: 50mm dynamic drivers with neodymium magnets deliver accurate lows, clear mids, and detailed highs so you can mix and edit with confidence
- Closed-back over-ear design: padded leatherette ear cushions and a reinforced adjustable headband isolate ambient noise and stay comfortable through 8 hour sessions
- Single-sided detachable cable: 3 m straight cable plugs into either ear cup for easy left or right routing and avoids tangling on your interface or laptop
- Universal connectivity: 3.5mm plug works with phones, tablets, and laptops; included screw-on 6.35mm (1/4 inch) adapter connects to studio interfaces, mixers, and amps
- In the box: Ally Studio 50 headphones, detachable 3 m cable, 6.35mm screw-on adapter, soft carrying pouch, and a 2 year limited warranty

**Rationale (conversion):** Each bullet answers a question shoppers ask before they buy: *will it sound good for what I do? will it be comfortable for long sessions? will it work with my gear? what do I actually get?* The included-accessories + warranty bullet is the single highest-impact lever for consumer electronics — it removes the "what am I getting in the box" hesitation that drives carts to abandon.

**Competitor reference:** Audio-Technica gets spec density right (40mm drivers, copper-clad aluminum voice coils, circumaural) but skips benefit framing; Philips nails benefit framing ("Amplify Your Listening Experience with powerful 40mm drivers that consistently deliver crisp, clear sound") but buries specs in marketing prose. The proposed bullets combine both and add what neither competitor does: an explicit accessories/warranty bullet — the CE category guide's recommended bullet 5.

**Amazon rule citations:**
- Highlight the top 5 features ([Listings PDF](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf))
- Begin each bullet with a capital letter; write fragments without ending punctuation ([Listings PDF](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf))
- Separate phrases within a bullet with semicolons ([Listings PDF](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf))
- Recommended electronics bullet structure — synopsis → materials → major feature → minor feature → included accessories/warranty ([CE Style Guide PDF](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf))
- Don't list features without benefits ([CE Style Guide PDF](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf))
- Write all numbers as numerals ([CE Style Guide PDF](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf))

> Note on punctuation: bullets above use commas and a colon. The Amazon Listings PDF's strict reading is "no hyphens, symbols, periods, or exclamation points" — commas and colons are not explicitly prohibited and are widely used by every live competitor sampled (OneOdio, Audio-Technica, Philips).

### Edit 3 — Expand the description to cover dimensions, use cases, and warranty

**Before:**

> Professional-style wired headphones for studio use and home listening.

**After:**

> The Ally Studio 50 is a closed-back, over-ear wired headphone designed for studio monitoring, podcast and content recording, and critical home listening.
