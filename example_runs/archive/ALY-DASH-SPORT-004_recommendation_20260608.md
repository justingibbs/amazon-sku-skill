## Approved edits

### Edit 1 — Rewrite the title

**Before**
> Ally Dash Sport Earbuds

**After**
> Ally Dash Sport Wireless Earbuds, Bluetooth 5 In-Ear Headphones with Ear Hooks, IPX5 Sweatproof, 30 Hour Battery, for Running, Gym, and Workout

**Rationale (search ranking).** The current title is 23 characters and carries only "sport" and "earbuds" as searchable terms. The rewrite — 144 characters, under Amazon's 200-char cap — adds the high-volume attributes shoppers actually query: "wireless," "Bluetooth," "ear hooks," "IPX5 sweatproof," battery hours, and the three top use-cases. It also follows the Consumer Electronics title format Amazon recommends.

**Competitor reference.** All three competitor titles in this niche are attribute-stacked. HAOYUYAN's title (`Wireless Earbuds, Sports Bluetooth Headphones, LED Power Display ... 80Hrs Playtime, Noise Canceling Headset, IPX7 Waterproof Earphones for Workout/Running`) is the best structural model.

**Amazon rule citations**
- 200-character title cap, effective 2025-01-21 — <https://sellercentral.amazon.com/seller-forums/discussions/t/b2b15728-0d43-453e-974f-59eb63f73059>
- CE category recommended title format (Brand + Series + Model + Form Factor + "with" Unique Thing) — <https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf>
- Use numerals ("30" not "thirty"); abbreviate measurements where helpful — <https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf>

### Edit 2 — Replace 3 thin bullets with a 5-bullet, attribute-rich set

**Before**
- Great for running and the gym.
- Sweat resistant.
- Long battery life.

**After**
- True wireless Bluetooth 5 sport earbuds with secure-fit ear hooks; designed for running, HIIT, and gym workouts so they stay locked in during high-intensity movement
- 30 hour total playtime with 8 hours per charge plus 22 hours from the USB-C charging case; LED case display shows remaining battery at a glance
- IPX5 sweat and water resistant rating protects the earbuds during heavy training, outdoor runs, and rain
- 10mm dynamic drivers tuned for sport with deep bass and clear vocals; built-in microphone with noise reduction for clear hands-free calls
- Includes 3 silicone ear tip sizes, USB-C charging cable, quick-start guide, and 1 year Ally Audio warranty

> Note: substitute the actual specs — battery hours, driver size, IPX rating, included items, warranty term — with Ally's true values before publishing. The values above are template defaults.

**Rationale (search ranking).** Amazon indexes bullet text for keyword search. Adding numeric attributes (battery hours, IPX rating, driver mm) and use-case terms ("HIIT," "running," "outdoor," "hands-free calls") expands the listing's query coverage substantially. The current bullets carry zero numbers and zero attribute keywords. The new set also pairs each feature with a benefit, per CE category guidance.

**Competitor reference.** JLab Go Sport+ leads bullet 1 with the battery spec ("9+ Hours Per Earbud, Plus 26+ More From The Case") and uses bullet 5 for warranty — the structure Amazon's CE Style Guide recommends. HAOYUYAN uses 6 bullets covering fit, sound, charging, battery, pairing, and sustainability.

**Amazon rule citations**
- Highlight top 5 features; pair each feature with a benefit; warranty + accessories as last bullet — <https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf>
- Begin each bullet with a capital letter, write fragments (no ending punctuation), use numerals, avoid vague statements — <https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf>

**Rationale (search ranking).** Amazon's own guidance says to "describe major product features such as size, style, and what the product can be used for" and to include warranty info. The current 84-char description does none of that, missing every searchable attribute. A fuller text description is especially valuable here because all three competitors use A+ Content for their descriptions — the text-description field is an under-contested surface to rank in.
