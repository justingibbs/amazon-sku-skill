# Amazon Content Guidelines (Compiled)

> Compiled from Amazon's public Seller Central help pages, Amazon-published Category Style Guide PDFs, and the Amazon Listings Product Detail Page Guide PDF. Last fetched: 2026-06-07.
>
> Where Amazon's own documents conflict (most often because newer Seller Central pages have updated limits from older PDF style guides), both versions are listed and dated. When writing or auditing a listing, the newer Seller Central rule should generally win.

## 1. Product Titles

- Titles for most product categories may not exceed 200 characters, including spaces (effective January 21, 2025). [source](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/seller-forums/discussions/t/b2b15728-0d43-453e-974f-59eb63f73059)
- Amazon recommends keeping titles to fewer than 80 characters. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- The special characters `!`, `$`, `?`, `_`, `{`, `}`, `^`, `¬`, and `¦` are not allowed in titles unless they are part of the brand name. [source](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/seller-forums/discussions/t/b2b15728-0d43-453e-974f-59eb63f73059)
- Titles may not contain the same word more than twice; prepositions, articles, and conjunctions are exceptions. [source](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/seller-forums/discussions/t/b2b15728-0d43-453e-974f-59eb63f73059)
- Do not use ALL CAPS in titles. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Capitalize the first letter of each word except for prepositions (in, on, over, with), conjunctions (and, or, for), and articles (the, a, an). [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Use numerals: write "2" instead of "two". [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Do not use non-language ASCII characters such as Æ, ©, or ®. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Titles should contain the minimal information needed to identify the item and nothing more. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Do not use subjective commentary in titles, such as "Hot Item" or "Best Seller". [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Titles can include necessary punctuation: hyphens (-), forward slashes (/), commas (,), ampersands (&), and periods (.). [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Titles can abbreviate measurements such as "cm", "oz", "in", and "kg". [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Do not include the merchant (seller) name in titles. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Recommended Consumer Electronics title format: Brand + Series + Model Name + Model Number [if applicable] + Form Factor + "with" Unique Thing [if applicable] + (color, packaging [if applicable]). [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Begin titles with the brand name; then series, model, what the item is (e.g., "Headphones"), measurements, color, and packaging in parentheses separated by commas. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Do not include condition notes such as "Refurbished" or "Remanufactured" in the title. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Do not include stock numbers or UPC codes in a title. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Do not include terms like "Free", "Bonus", "Price", "Exclusive", or "A $XX Value" in titles. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Do not include marketing terms that are not part of the product's trademarked name (e.g., "Stylish Lightweight Fontopia Headphones" should read "Fontopia Headphones"). [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Size information should only be included in titles when it differentiates the product from similar products. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Color is generally not necessary in the title unless it is a special feature of the product (e.g., a limited edition). [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Do not use the term "OEM" in your title (or elsewhere) because it might be confusing to customers. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- If a model number is included, place it at the end in parentheses; do not start with the model number. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Spell out measurement words (inches, feet, pounds) rather than using symbols such as `"` — for example use "52-Inch" not `52"`; symbols hurt free-text search. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Conflict note — older (2018) Consumer Electronics Style Guide sets a 150-character title limit and a 256-character bullet limit; the 2025 Seller Central policy supersedes the title limit with the 200-character rule. Use the newer rule. [older source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf) / [newer source](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/seller-forums/discussions/t/b2b15728-0d43-453e-974f-59eb63f73059)

## 2. Bullet Points (Key Product Features)

- Highlight the top five features you want customers to consider (e.g., dimensions, age appropriateness, country of origin). [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Begin each bullet point with a capital letter. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Write in fragments and do not include ending punctuation. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Write all numbers as numerals. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Separate phrases within one bullet with semicolons. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Spell out measurements such as litres and metres in bullets. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Do not use hyphens, symbols, periods, or exclamation points in bullets. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Do not write vague statements; be as specific as possible about features and attributes. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- The CE category guidance is to make each bullet brief yet descriptive, not paragraph-length. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Don't just list features — pair each feature with a benefit. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Include warranty information and included accessories as the last bullet point. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Recommended bullet structure for electronics like headphones: Bullet 1 = overall synopsis; Bullet 2 = materials and construction; Bullet 3 = major feature(s); Bullet 4 = minor feature(s); Bullet 5 = included accessories / warranty. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Always use numerals; never spell out numbers in bullets. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Conflict note — the 2018 CE Style Guide gives a 256-character bullet limit while current Seller Central guidance for most third-party sellers indicates roughly 200–500 characters per bullet depending on category and seller type; check the live category template for the exact figure. [older source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)

## 3. Product Descriptions

- Describe major product features such as size, style, and what the product can be used for. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Include accurate dimensions, care instructions, and warranty information. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Use correct grammar, punctuation, and complete sentences. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Do not include seller name, e-mail address, website URL, company-specific information, or details about another product you sell. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Do not include HTML, JavaScript, or other executables in detail pages, with the exception of break tags (`</br>`) used for line breaks. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Do not mention competitors or other brands by name in descriptions; if making performance comparisons, compare against established averages or maximums for the product type. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Do not mention your own offer details (e.g., "free shipping"). [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Avoid ALL CAPS and multiple exclamation points in running text and graphical elements. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Colored or bold text should never be used for emphasis in running text; use italics instead. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Leave trademark (™) and registered trademark (®) symbols out of the catalog; ASCII symbols and HTML entities like `&reg;` are not supported. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Use the percent symbol (%) and ampersand (&) in feature bullets and technical specs, but spell them out in product titles and descriptions. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)

## 4. Prohibited Promotional / Subjective Claims

- Reviews, quotes, or testimonials are prohibited on detail pages (titles, descriptions, bullets, or images). [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Solicitations for positive customer reviews are prohibited. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Advertisements, promotional material, or watermarks on images, photos, or videos are prohibited. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Time-sensitive information (e.g., dates of promotional tours, seminars, lectures) is prohibited in titles, descriptions, bullets, or images. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Availability, price, condition, alternative ordering information (e.g., links to other websites for placing orders), or alternative delivery offers (such as free delivery) are prohibited. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Do not include seller-specific information such as price points, delivery speeds, or personal information. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Specifically banned title terms include "Free", "Bonus", "Price", "Exclusive", and "A $XX Value". [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Subjective commentary such as "Hot Item" or "Best Seller" is prohibited in titles. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Pornographic, obscene, or offensive content is prohibited in titles, descriptions, bullets, or images. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)

## 5. Contact Info and External References

- Phone numbers, physical mail addresses, email addresses, and website URLs are prohibited in detail page titles, descriptions, bullet points, or images. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Links to other websites for placing orders (and any alternative ordering information) are prohibited. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Do not include seller name, e-mail address, website URL, or company-specific information in product descriptions. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)

## 6. Main Image Requirements

- The MAIN image must show only the product for sale on a pure white background; the product should fill the image frame. [source](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/external/G1881?locale=en-US)
- MAIN images must show products outside of their packaging; boxes, bags, or cases should not appear unless they are an important product feature. [source](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/external/G1881?locale=en-US)
- MAIN images must not include text, logos, borders, color blocks, watermarks, or other graphics over the product or in the background. [source](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/external/G1881?locale=en-US)
- The product must fill at least 85% of the image. [source](https://sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/external/G1881?locale=en-US)
- All product images must meet Amazon's general image standards as well as any applicable category-specific image guidelines. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Images must accurately represent the product that is for sale and must match the product title. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)

## 7. Image File Technical Requirements

- Optimal zoom experience requires files of at least 1600 px on the longest side; the smallest a file can be for zoom is 1000 px, and the smallest for the site is 500 px. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Images must not exceed 10,000 px on the longest side. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Accepted formats are JPEG (.jpg or .jpeg), TIFF (.tif), PNG (.png), and GIF (.gif). JPEG is preferred. Animated GIFs are not supported. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Images must not be blurry, pixelated, or have jagged edges. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Consumer Electronics primary image guidance: provide a .jpg or .tif at least 1000 x 1000 px and up to 3000 x 3000 px to take full advantage of zoom. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- For white products, use a drop shadow to offset the product from the white background. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Sellers can upload up to 8 alternate images in addition to the MAIN image (Consumer Electronics). [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)
- Alternate images should feature the product in use and close-ups of important features. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)

## 8. Detail Page Creation and Catalog Rules

- All products must be appropriately and accurately categorized. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Product titles, descriptions, and bullet points must be clearly written and assist the customer in understanding the product. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- The use of false product identification information — including UPC codes or publication dates — in product detail pages is prohibited. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Creating a product detail page for a product that already exists in Amazon's catalog is prohibited; match to the existing ASIN instead. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Using product detail pages to cross-merchandise or cross-promote a different product is prohibited. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Detail pages may not feature or contain Restricted Products. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- Do not update an existing listing for a new version of a product (changes in color, size, material, features, or product name require a new ASIN). [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)

## 9. Brand and Manufacturer Identification

- Do not use your seller name as the Brand or Manufacturer unless the product is Private Label. [source](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/35/sp-marketing-toolkit/Sellerfacingguides/Amazon_Listings_Product_Detail_Page_Guide.pdf)
- For accessories, use the brand name of the manufacturer; do not use the brand of a compatible base device (e.g., do not list "Dell" as the brand of a generic Dell-compatible battery). Use "Generic" for generic products. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)

## 10. Customer Reviews Submitted by Sellers

- It is never acceptable to comment negatively about a competitor in a customer review that you have written. [source](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/G/01/rainier/help/CEStyleGuide.pdf)

### Notes on source quality

- The two best primary sources for this skill are Amazon's own PDF Style Guides (CE Style Guide and the Listings Product Detail Page Guide), because their full text is publicly accessible and stable.
- Most `sellercentral.amazon.com/help/hub/reference/external/...` pages require a Seller Central login or render via JavaScript that strips content from plain fetchers; cite them by ID but be aware the text shown above is mirrored from Amazon's own Seller Forums announcement and Amazon-published PDFs.
- All rules above were taken from Amazon-published documents. Where the older CE Style Guide PDF conflicts with newer Seller Central pages (notably the title character limit), both are listed with their sources and the newer rule should prevail.
